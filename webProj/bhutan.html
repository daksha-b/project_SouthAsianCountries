BHUTAN

Nestled within the embrace of the mighty Himalayas, Bhutan emerges as a serene jewel of the East. This small kingdom, often referred to as the "Land of the Thunder Dragon," captivates with its pristine landscapes, spiritual richness, and commitment to preserving its unique cultural heritage. Bhutan's traditional architecture, adorned with vibrant prayer flags fluttering in the breeze, creates a picturesque scene against the backdrop of snow-capped peaks and lush valleys.
