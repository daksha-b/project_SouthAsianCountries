BANGLADESH

Bangladesh, a land of lush greenery and riverine beauty, is a country brimming with resilience and vibrancy. Nestled in the heart of the Bengal Delta, it boasts a rich tapestry of culture, history, and natural wonders. From the bustling streets of Dhaka to the tranquil countryside dotted with emerald rice paddies, Bangladesh offers a unique blend of urban excitement and rural serenity.
