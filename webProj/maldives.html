MALDIVES

The Maldives, a tropical paradise scattered across the Indian Ocean, entices travelers with its postcard-perfect scenery and unparalleled luxury. Comprising over a thousand coral islands, this archipelago nation boasts pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life. Each island resort offers a secluded haven of relaxation and indulgence, where guests can unwind in luxurious overwater bungalows and experience world-class amenities amidst breathtaking natural beauty.
