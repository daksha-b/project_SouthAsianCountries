NEPAL

Nepal, nestled in the embrace of the Himalayas, is a land of unparalleled beauty and spiritual serenity. Home to the world's highest peaks, including Mount Everest, this small yet diverse country captivates adventurers and spiritual seekers alike. Its ancient temples, monasteries, and stupas dot the landscape, bearing witness to centuries of rich cultural heritage and religious devotion. From the bustling streets of Kathmandu to the tranquil valleys of Pokhara, Nepal's diverse landscapes offer endless opportunities for exploration and discovery.
